The All Star Difference

WHY CHOOSE ALL STAR?

From the first consultation to long-term maintenance, everything we do is built around efficiency, durability, and your complete satisfaction.

Trusted by homeowners & businessesacross Pineville, Alexandria & Central LA
We design every system with the latest in smart irrigation — efficient controllers, precision spray and rotor heads, and trenchless installation that minimizes disruption while maximizing coverage. The result is a system that waters smarter, not harder.
We never cut corners on components. From durable piping to commercial-grade heads and valves, we use only the highest quality materials so your system delivers reliable, efficient performance season after season.
Our customer-focused approach guarantees every project is completed to the highest standards. We listen first, design around your property's unique needs, and stay with you from consultation and design through installation and ongoing maintenance.
A healthy, vibrant landscape starts with water delivered exactly where and when it's needed. Our designs optimize water usage to promote optimal growth for your lawn and garden — saving water and keeping your landscape lush.
Whether it's a backyard or a large commercial property, our team has the experience to scale. We deliver customized sprinkler systems built to the unique demands of each project, large or small.
